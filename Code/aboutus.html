About US



An application to be developed for Cool-Finland Oy to manage the handling capacity of refrigeration equipment, which their customers should be able to access and see, and to reserve for which day they will deliver refrigeration equipment (for recycling). Cool-Finland can handle 40,000 tons of refrigeration equipment per day, but they sometimes receive 90,000 tons or more per day, which cannot fit in their premises - meaning the trucks have to wait for long hours in the yard until they can unload the refrigeration equipment at the plant.
